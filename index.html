About Us

Quality Meat from Farm to Table

At Vlace, we're passionate about connecting people with premium quality bulk beef and pork sourced locally from small family farms and butcher shops. In doing so - we will unveil the source of our food so the customer knows where it came from, just how it used to be.

Our platform was created to share our passion for a superior way to buy meat—locally and in bulk. This approach offers a convenient, high-quality, and valuable source of protein

Our Process

Easy Listing + Purchasing

Farm Owner

Easily input your farm details, list livestock and start receiving instant bookings

List a farm

Signup by selecting "Become a Farmer" in the upper right of the screen. Easily add details such as farm images, livestock, butcher and more! Manage listings, pricing, and notifications form your farm's site!"

Follow Order

After a customer books, receive automated emails after with their contact information.

Process Order

Process and deliver orders to the butcher as normal. Inform Admin of final wieght and price and let the system automatically bill the customer!

Chashout

Once Final Payment is completed by the customer the payment will be sent directly to Farmer.

Market Place Customers

Effortlessly find products, farms

Search Farm or Farmer

Easily search for farms, farmers and more!

Reserve Your Meat

After a customer books, receive automated emails after with their contact information.

Make Final Payment

Once livestock is taken in, an automated email will be sent with final payment weight and price per pound. Pay the remaining balance by Credit Card minus the deposit before pickup!

Pick Your Order

An automated email will be sent when the order is ready for pickup. Head to the butcher and collect your order!
